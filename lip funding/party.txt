Barnet: Conservative to Labour (2022).

Harrow: Labour to Conservative (2022).

Kingston upon Thames: Conservative to Liberal Democrat (2018).

Richmond upon Thames: Conservative to Liberal Democrat (2018).

Tower Hamlets: NOC to Labour (2018), then Labour to Aspire (2022).

Wandsworth: Conservative to Labour (2022).

Westminster: Conservative to Labour (2022).
